Study OS — Local Worker Runner 🔥

Finishes onboarding from your own browser, with no Supabase cron and (almost) no hosted invocations. Each click of Run one step processes a small batch; Auto-run keeps going until the queue is empty.

This calls your worker the same way the cron would, but from here — so it isn't blocked by Supabase's hosted invocation cap. It runs one batch every few seconds. Watch your course's Activity feed fill in. When the log says queue empty a few times in a row, onboarding is done — you can close this tab. Your secret never leaves your browser.
